We are e-Shop

At e-Shop we have a clear purpose – to empower millions of people across the globe by helping them connect and share their goods and services. This inspiring purpose has helped us build one of the world’s leading online classifieds companies. Today, we are serving over 240 million unique users every month, generating over 11 billion monthly page views in more than 40 countries. If you’ve got an entrepreneurial drive and think you can use that drive to contribute to our purpose, then we’d love to empower you to be successful.
